Chemical Achievements

Bonding Theory Revolution

Pauling’s resonance theory explained how electrons are shared across multiple atoms, like in benzene, which helps predict molecular stability and reactivity. This insight is essential in fields like drug design, where understanding how molecules interact is crucial.

Molecular Structure Insights

Pauling used X-ray crystallography to reveal how the arrangement of atoms in molecules like proteins affects their function. His work laid the groundwork for understanding molecular geometry and its impact on biological activity.

Discovery of Alpha-Helix & Beta-Sheet Structures

Pauling’s discovery of the alpha-helix and beta-sheet structures in proteins highlighted the role of hydrogen bonds in stabilizing protein folding. This breakthrough provided key insights into how protein structure determines function.

Medical Contribution

The Vitamin C Enthusiast

Linus Pauling promoted vitamin C as a cure for the common cold, suggesting high doses could boost immunity. His claims sparked debate and further research into its health benefits.
